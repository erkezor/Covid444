How to protect yourself from coronavirus? 6 rules of strong immunity

1. Monitor the air circulation

To reduce the likelihood of COVID-19 infection, it is necessary to reduce the concentration of pathogens in the air. It is from this that the probability of illness largely depends. Therefore, enhanced ventilation is an excellent solution!

2. Strengthen the body with vitamins

It is very important in the midst of diseases to strengthen the body with vitamins and trace elements that allow you to support the body's defenses

3. Master breathing exercises

Doctors advise to focus on exercises that are aimed at deep breathing. They improve the patency of the respiratory tract. Exercises to strengthen the respiratory muscles are also important.

4. Keep an eye on intestinal health

The gastrointestinal tract is constantly in contact with the external environment (as food enters it), and when food is absorbed, the intestines must distinguish useful food components from infectious and toxic ones. Protection from the effects of external adverse factors, including potential carcinogens, is carried out with the help of an effective barrier function of the intestine and the system of natural (innate) and acquired immunity.

5. Get enough sleep

Lack of sleep impairs brain function, reduces immunity, increases the susceptibility to colds and the risk of developing problems with the cardiovascular system, and also reduces the effectiveness of vaccination.

6. Be active

Specialists from the University of Iowa, USA, conducted an interesting experiment in 2009. They selected 3 groups of mice. The first group was not given any additional loads. The second one has been moderately "engaged" in physical activity for 14 weeks. The third made a 45-minute "run" before the experiment. After that, all the mice were infected with the flu virus.
